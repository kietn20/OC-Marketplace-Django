About

OC Marketplace is a community-driven platform that connects sellers and buyers through meaningful interactions and unique goods. Sellers and buyers are welcomed to negotiate the prices of their products to satisfy each other. This platform is made exclusively for the Orange County communities. Anyone can list their items with no hidden fees. Whether you're selling or buying, you are welcome to connect and interact with each other. Happy selling or buying! - Kite
